# Adaire Chat Onboarding Guide

## Open Adaire Chat and on the login screen, tap Sign in manually.
<img src="https://github.com/helloadaire/AdaireChatSynapseInterception/blob/main/assets/user-guide-01.png?raw=true" alt="User guide screenshot" style="max-width: 450px; width: 450px;" />

## Tap Change account provider.
<img src="https://github.com/helloadaire/AdaireChatSynapseInterception/blob/main/assets/user-guide-02.png?raw=true" alt="User guide screenshot" style="max-width: 450px; width: 450px;" />

## Select “Adaire Chat” as the account provider.
<img src="https://github.com/helloadaire/AdaireChatSynapseInterception/blob/main/assets/user-guide-03.png?raw=true" alt="User guide screenshot" style="max-width: 450px; width: 450px;" />

## Enter the provider domain:
### synapse.production-messaging.adaire.dev
<img src="https://github.com/helloadaire/AdaireChatSynapseInterception/blob/main/assets/user-guide-04.png?raw=true" alt="User guide screenshot" style="max-width: 450px; width: 450px;" />

## Tap Continue.
<img src="https://github.com/helloadaire/AdaireChatSynapseInterception/blob/main/assets/user-guide-05.png?raw=true" alt="User guide screenshot" style="max-width: 450px; width: 450px;" />

## Enter your username and password, then tap Continue.
<img src="https://github.com/helloadaire/AdaireChatSynapseInterception/blob/main/assets/user-guide-pwd.png?raw=true" alt="User guide screenshot" style="max-width: 450px; width: 450px;" />

## Tap Enter recovery key.
<img src="https://github.com/helloadaire/AdaireChatSynapseInterception/blob/main/assets/user-guide-07.png?raw=true" alt="User guide screenshot" style="max-width: 450px; width: 450px;" />

## Input your recovery key and tap Continue.
<img src="https://github.com/helloadaire/AdaireChatSynapseInterception/blob/main/assets/user-guide-08.png?raw=true" alt="User guide screenshot" style="max-width: 450px; width: 450px;" />

## On the Device Verified screen, tap Continue.
<img src="https://github.com/helloadaire/AdaireChatSynapseInterception/blob/main/assets/user-guide-9.png?raw=true" alt="User guide screenshot" style="max-width: 450px; width: 450px;" />
